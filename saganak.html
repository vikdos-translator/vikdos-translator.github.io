Analysis

Sağanak is a song co-written and sung by Turkish music artist Aspova (real name: Muhammed Kılıçaslan). The song is written in pop-rhythm style. The narrator in the song tells of his parting with the person he had a relationship with.

A downpour (sağanak) is used as a metaphor for the memories which wash down the 'buildings' of the narrator's thoughts. The narrator also says "I thought that the memories went away, but they're still here", which provides more support for the interpretation.

Although the song is relatively repetitive, the lyrics and tone of the singer, not to mention the accompanying instrumentation, allow the listener to imagine the 'downpour' which the narrator experiences and adds to the meaning of the song.
