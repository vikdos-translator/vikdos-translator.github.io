Analysis

Я люблю тебя (Ya lublyu tebya) is a song written and sung by Russian music group and Azerbaijani brothers Rauf & Faik. In particular, the song highlights the main character's inability to confirm his love for the girl he shares a relationship with. In particular, he is unable to say the words "I love you".

In the music video, the main character (Faik) breaks up with the girl he has a relationship with. This girl moves on to someone else, who happens to be Faik's brother, Rauf. Although the girl loves Rauf, she is unable to move past her love for Faik, which upsets Rauf. Yet Faik's inability to utter the words "I love you" at the end of the video is what makes her move on and accept the love which Rauf has for her.

Yet even though Faik can't say the words "I love you", in the song he does acknowledge that he loves the girl. And he regrets that he wasn't able to say the words, and that he might not be able to get her back. With the end of the video, we see that Faik won't ever be able to get her back, and that the girl has fully accepted Rauf's love.
