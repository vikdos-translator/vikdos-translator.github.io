Analysis

Унесённые Ветрами (Unesonniye Vetrami) is a song written and performed by Russian music group and Azerbaijani brothers Rauf & Faik. The song reflects on the firm determination within each of our souls to continue life, even with its various misfortunes.

The song opens with the lines "My friend, I'm in love again, easy peasy lemon squeezy, now and then." From the beginning of the song, it is clear that the narrator has gone through previous relationships and heartbreaks, and thus the commencement of a new relationship is bittersweet: a new chance for love, and a new chance for heartbreak.

The lines "Let's hear together what the sky tells us, how many airplanes the wind took away today, we don't have money only inhibitions" reflect on the hardship of human existence, that life is not the perfect dream that we imagine, instead it is broken by heartbreak and the things that we can't have. Yet the following lines "But today let's pretend it's the opposite, and all that there is will replace us with silence, as it happens in books a sunny July morning, my soul feels so warm and cozy" show that the narrators of the song are not at all affected by their broken dream, as they know that in their imagination they can still remember a dream that is beautiful and unbroken.

The lines "I tossed up my money so high, my soul feels so clean and light, and all the planes are so far away, ok" reflect on the fact that the narrators have done away with the constraints of their existence, they have thrown away the money which they had so little of anyway and they accept that the "planes" are gone, never to come back - the "planes" of memories, of people, of the things which happened, and things which couldn't.

The lines of the chorus "And the planes gone with the wind, thousands of kilometers, and whoever did not share their secrets, fly far away!" reflect on the narrator's experiences of hardship and pain, and their wish for those who cannot share or contemplate their experiences to "fly far away".

Yet there is something about the song that can never be described in words, that describes the beauty of life, the happiness and sadness, the will in each of us to survive, to move on despite all the suffering and all the loss.
